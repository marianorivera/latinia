Artificial Intelligence Workshop 2025

Connecting Mexican & Latin American AI Scientists

October 29-31, 2025 ? Guanajuato, Mexico

About the Workshop

This 3-day workshop aims to bring together Mexican and Latin American scientists actively researching Artificial Intelligence. Join us in the beautiful city of Guanajuato to share ideas, foster collaborations, and explore the latest advances in AI research.

The event will feature keynote talks, panel discussions, hands-on sessions, and networking opportunities to build a strong AI community across Latin America.
